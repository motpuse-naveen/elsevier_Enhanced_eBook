Mitosis in a cell

Video 1.1: Mitosis in a cell with fluorescently labelled chromosomes and microtubules

Time lapse video of a human epithelial cancer cell line (HeLa) with Histone H2B in red (labelling chromosomes), and alpha tubulin in green (labelling spindle microtubules). There is one frame every three minutes.
